cryptedmail

Create clean @cryptedmail.com addresses for every part of your life, keep them in one familiar mailbox, and burn any address the moment it starts leaking, tracking, or collecting spam. cryptedmail keeps message content off-chain and uses blockchain-backed receipts for ownership, plan access, public keys, and deactivation proof.

Features

Disposable privacy that moves faster than leaks.

cryptedmail is built around quick exits, not permanent aliases. Keep normal email, separate every identity, lock encrypted messages to the sender and receiver, and burn an exposed address before it becomes your problem.
